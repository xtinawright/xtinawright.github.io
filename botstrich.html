The Bot-Strich: 2022

The task: Using 8 motors, a raspberry pi and a budget of $100, build a two legged walking robot.

For Robotics Studio taught by Hod Lipson, I built my robot with inspriation from an ostrich, with a backward bending knees design. I developed a parallel linkage system for the legs to keep the feet parallel to the ground. I also spent time digitally simulating the robot to use machine learning to develop better walk cycles. Currently, the robot has walked successfully.
