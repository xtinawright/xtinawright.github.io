The Bot-Strich

The Challenge:

Using 8 motors, a raspberry pi and a budget of $100, build a two legged walking robot.

I am built my robot to be inspired by a ostrich with a backward bending knees design. I developed a parallel linkage system for the legs to keep the feet parallel to the ground. We also spent time simulating the robots to use machine learning to develop better walk cycles. Currently, the robot has walked successfully.
