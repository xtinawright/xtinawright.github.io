Interactive Periodic Table: 2018

Patent number: US11,609,305

The Task: Create an educational tool to teach a chemistry concept.

Patent Issued: March 14th 2023. This periodic table has individual pieces for each element that move when placed on top of a piece with relative data represented in height to demonstrate different periodic trends. The models were designed in Fusion 360 and 3D printed.
