Video Game Project: 2019

The Task: Create an video game powered by an ardunio, using a $50 budget.

The hardware for this game was designed to play battleship. I configured the electronics, and designed and assembled the housing for the game. The other group members were responsible for the software of the game. Upon turning in the game, it functioned as an electronic etch-a-sketch.
