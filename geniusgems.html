Genius Gems Contributions 2020

In April of 2020, I joined Genius Gems. They are an eduational childrens playspace. Originally, I was tasked to repair several 3D printers and once I had their printer farm operational, I designed projects for small batch manufacturing for various activities and lessons. These projects were designed for ease of assembly, quick manufacturing time and efficent material use.
