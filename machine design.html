Machine Design Project: In Progress

The Task: Create four bar linkage that can quickly press the buttons on a game board.

This project is working in tandem to coursework covering the fundamentals and principles behind both mechanical and electromechanical systems. We are looking at linkages, motors, sensors and using the learned principles to design our linkages.

We are currenly machining the necessary pieces for our linkage, utlizing a water jet and 3D printing to create parts and finishing them in the machine shop.
