Machine Design Project: 2022

The Task: Create four bar linkage that can quickly press the buttons on a game board.

The buttons will light up in a random sequence created by the professor. Our linkage responds to a digital signal sent by an arduino and presses the corresponding button pair. We have 60 seconds to press as many button pairs as possible.

We are scored as follows:

  • 1 point for a correct pair
  • 3 points for an optional bonus button
  • -2 points for an incorrect button press

Our team had a final score of 192.

This project is working in tandem to coursework covering the fundamentals and principles behind both mechanical and electromechanical systems. We are looking at linkages, motors, encoders, sensors and using the learned principles to design our linkages. We used waterjetting, laser cutting and 3D printing to manufacture parts. We also used a CNC lathe and mill and a drill and arbor press for finishing processes.

Our linkage is connected to an ardunio and the motion is determined by PID control.
