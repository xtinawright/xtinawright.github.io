Senior Design Capstone Project: In Progress

The task: As a team we are building a grabbing mechanism to capture debris in Lower Earth Orbit.

Our team is working with NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory to design this mechanism. We plan to target objects with a diameter of 10-30 centimeters. This will allow us to build a full sized mechanism with the alloted time and resources. Also, there is no work is being done to capture objects of this size. Currently, we are building rough protoypes to determine geometry for our mechanism.

The first mechanism is made up of articulated petals that will actuate together. We want these petals to first open into a funnel that will envelope an object. Then the petals will fold in and contain the object. There is an aperature leading to the storage area that will open as the petals compress and push the debris into storage. We are planning for the petals to have a large number of joints to add more degrees of freedom in the motion from a single acutation.
