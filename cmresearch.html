Research Contributions

For summer of 2022, I assisted with the research of a PhD student in the Creative Machines Lab at Columbia. I was responsible for developing a specific part of a inverted laser sintering 3D printer. I will be continuing to contribute this fall.

This part will capture excess plastic powder for reuse, the part is adjustable to increase the piece's lifespan as the project develops. The ridges are used to agitate and remove additional powder.
