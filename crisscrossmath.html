Criss-Cross Math: 2021

The Task: Create a non-electrical toy that would teach a child addition and subtraction using Solidworks.

Our design consisted of laser cut wood and 3D printed elements that would hold a double sided answer sheet. A child could move the sliders to highlight an equation of their choice and the two sliders would outline the correct answer in the center. This answer sheet could be removed and flipped over to display answers for subtraction equations. We were directed to focus on the computational side of addition and subtraction rather than counting/physical side and we were challenged with making the toy “self checking”.
